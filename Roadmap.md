# Roadmap

Planned skills for Dugsiiye Skills, grouped by category. This is a working list — order, priority, and scope will change as we build.

## Payments & finance

- **EVC Plus integration** — Hormuud's API for sending/receiving payments, checking balance, transaction status
- **Zaad integration** — Telesom's mobile money (Somaliland)
- **Sahal integration** — Golis
- **eDahab integration** — Somtel
- **Premier Wallet / MyBank / Salaam Bank APIs** — local bank transfers
- **Cross-wallet transfers** — handling the messy reality of EVC ↔ Zaad ↔ bank
- **Somali invoice formatter** — invoices that include the right wallet numbers, VAT (where applicable), and bilingual labels
- **Hawala transfer formatting** — Dahabshiil, Amal, Tawakal money transfer records

## Language & writing

- **Somali formal writing** — warqado rasmi ah, warbixino, qoraal shaqo
- **English ↔ Somali translation** — tuned for Somali idioms, not literal word-swaps
- **Maay / Maxaa tiri dialect handling**
- **Somali transliteration** — Arabic-script Somali ↔ Latin-script Somali
- **Religious text formatting** — Qur'anic verses, hadith citations, Arabic interleaving

## Documents & government

- **Somali CV / résumé builder** — formatted for local employers and diaspora job markets
- **Business registration documents** — for Somalia, Somaliland, Puntland
- **Rental agreements, contracts** — bilingual templates
- **NGO / aid project proposals** — the format donors expect for Somalia-based projects

## Local context

- **Somali address formatting** — degmo, xaafad, landmark-based
- **Somali phone number validation** — +252, +252-63 (Somaliland), carrier detection
- **Hijri ↔ Gregorian calendar** — with Somali month names
- **Somali name parsing** — three-name structure (magaca, magaca aabaha, magaca awoowaha)
- **Prayer times for Somali cities** — Mogadishu, Hargeisa, Kismayo, Bosaso, Garowe

## Education

- **Dugsi curriculum helpers** — Qur'an memorization tracking, tajweed
- **Somali school subjects** — aligned with the national curriculum
- **Diaspora kids learning Somali** — graded materials for heritage learners

## Commerce & logistics

- **Bakaaro Market price lookups / formatting**
- **Shipping to Somalia** — customs forms, freight forwarder workflows
